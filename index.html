About Me

自我介紹

I am Fiona Lam and I am studying at Colégio Diocesano de São José Secondary School. I am the only child in my family, so my parents have high expectations of me, but they do not expect me to achieve anything, they just teach me to do my best in everything I do. They both had a high level of awareness and created a good environment for me to grow up in.

My father always said to me that of all the mistakes we make, the one we can easily forgive is laziness! He was so strict with me that when I grew up, I was very disciplined, my character would give me success and my hobbies would enrich my life. So, apart from my studies, I was also very interested in music. I learned piano from a young age and participated in many piano competitions with good results. I also love all kinds of sports and I am a keen table tennis player. I have won many inter-class table tennis competitions in secondary school and joined the school handball team.

I'm a person who loves challenge and always interested to learn new things. I see a problem as a challenge to make me me grow as a person. I always try to break my limit and eliminate the mental wall. Stay brave is my motto.

我是林淑慧，就讀於聖若瑟教區中學第五校英文部。我是家中的獨女，所以父母對我的寄望很大，可是他們並不要求我要達到什麼成就，他們只是教導我，在做每一件事情的時候都要盡最大的努力。他們都有著較高的思想覺悟，為我創造了良好的成長環境。

爸爸經常對我說一句話：在所有的過錯中，我們最易於原諒的就是懶散！他對我的處事態度很嚴格，以至於長大之後，我也十分自律，我的性格會賦予我成功，我的愛好會豐富我的生活。因此，除了學習以外，我也對音樂十分感興趣，從小我就學習鋼琴，並參加過不少的鋼琴比賽，也獲得了不錯的成績。同時，我喜歡各種運動，並且熱愛乒乓球，在中學的多次班際兵乓球比賽中都獲得了冠軍，更加入了學校的手球隊。

我是一個喜歡挑戰的人，總是對學習新事物感興趣。我把問題看作是一種挑戰，讓我成長的機會。我總是試圖打破我的極限，消除心理障礙。我的座右銘是做人要堅強，要勇敢。
